CYBERSECURITY BREACHES
Cybersecurity breaches occur when attackers gain unauthorized access to computer systems or networks. These breaches can cause financial losses, service disruptions, and data leaks.

 RECENT CYBERSECURITY BREACHES

1. MGM RESORTS CYBERATTACK(2023)

CAUSE:
Hackers used social engineering to trick IT support into giving them access to internal systems.

IMPACT:
- Hotel systems stopped working
- Online bookings were disrupted
- Customer data may have been exposed
- Estimated losses exceeded $100 million

LESSON LEARNED:
Companies should use multi-factor authentication (MFA) and improve employee cybersecurity awareness.

___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

2. CHANGE HEALTHCARE RANSOMWARE (2024)

CAUSE:
Attackers deployed ransomware after gaining access to internal systems.

IMPACT:
- Healthcare services were disrupted
- Insurance billing systems stopped working
- Patient data was compromised

LESSON LEARNED:
Organizations should maintain system backups and update security software regularly.

________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

3. MICROSOFT EMAIL BREACH (2024)

CAUSE:
Hackers accessed email systems through a compromised account.

IMPACT:
- Sensitive emails were exposed
- Government and corporate data was accessed
- Raised concerns about cloud security

LESSON LEARNED
Companies should implement stronger identity verification and monitor account activity.
