PHISHING EMAIL IDENTIFICATION:

WHAT IS PHISHING?
Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers send fake emails pretending to be trusted organizations to steal sensitive information such as passwords or banking details.

COMMON SIGNS OF PHISHING EMAILS:
- Suspicious sender email address
- Urgent or threatening language
- Unexpected attachments
- Links to fake websites
- Requests for personal or financial information

TOOLS USED:

1.GOOGLE PHISHING QUIZ
https://phishingquiz.withgoogle.com

This tool helps users practice identifying phishing emails by showing examples of real and fake emails.

EXAMPLE of Phishing Email

Subject: Urgent Account Verification

Message:
Your bank account has been temporarily suspended. Please click the link below to verify your account immediately

Phishing Indicators
- Urgent request
- Suspicious link
- Requests personal information

CONCLUSION: This email is a phishing attempt.
